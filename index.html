What We're About

Unplug & Breathe is a digital well-being initiative rooted in the belief that everyone — especially young people growing up in a hyperconnected world — deserves the inner skills to navigate screens, social media, and constant stimulation with awareness and intention.

Through yoga, breathwork, meditation, and guided reflection, we create spaces where preteens, teens, and adults can pause, reconnect with themselves, and build healthier relationships with technology.

Based in Finland, we work with schools, families, and communities to bring these practices to those who need them most.

Yoga & Movement

Reconnecting with the body through gentle, mindful movement that grounds us in the present moment.

Breathwork

Simple, powerful breathing techniques that calm the nervous system and sharpen focus — skills for life.

Meditation & Reflection

Cultivating awareness and intention so we can choose how we relate to technology, not just react.

Welcome to Unplug & Breathe — a space to pause, reconnect, and find balance in a world that rarely slows down.

My journey began with a deep curiosity about the human mind and how we make sense of our inner and outer worlds. I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Linguistics and a Master’s Degree in Cognitive Science, with a focus on psychology and philosophy, both from the University of Barcelona. Alongside my academic path, I completed a three-year training in Yoga, Meditation, and Personal Stress Management Coaching through Shri Vivek Yoga Center (Barcelona) and Nirpeksha Sannyas Ashram (India).

I am also the founder of the grassroots movement Smartphone Free Childhood in Finland, where I advocate for a more conscious and balanced relationship with technology, especially for young people and families.

For the past 15 years, I’ve worked with adults, teens, and children across Spain, Canada, the United States, and Finland. My work brings together science and embodied practice — integrating yoga, breathwork, meditation, and creativity to support digital well-being in a practical and human way.

I guide people to make a more intentional and mindful use of technology — by creating space to breathe, reflect, and reconnect with what truly matters.

Digital Well-Being in Schools

Our school workshops create a space for students to pause, reflect, and better understand their digital habits. With a thoughtful and non-judgmental approach, we help young people feel more in control of how they use technology and focus on what truly matters.

Through a blend of yoga-inspired techniques, breathwork, meditation, open discussion, and creative expression, students are guided on a personal journey inward — learning to listen to their own needs, explore friendships in a more real and meaningful way, and strengthen their focus and concentration.

These workshops equip students with resilience, agency, and clarity, empowering them to make conscious choices and build a healthier, more intentional relationship with technology.
